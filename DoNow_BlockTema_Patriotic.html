Topic: This Is Our Block.

Imagine you and your neighbors on your block finally got rid of the old landlord who was controlling everything. You are all running your own spots now, your own way.

Then that same powerful landlord, from way across town, starts trying to come back. He is not bothering your house (yet), but he is trying to barge in and take over your neighbors' spots, right next to you.

QUESTION:
WHAT IS THE PUBLIC STATEMENT YOU MAKE?
WHAT IS THE NEW RULE YOU LAY DOWN, LOUD AND CLEAR, TO KEEP HIS HANDS OFF YOUR ENTIRE BLOCK?
