The P.A.G.E. Protocol

  • P = Prior knowledge: What do I know already?
  • A = Ask: What is being asked?
  • G = Gist: What does the doc say?
  • E = Eliminate: What is not correct?

P = Prior Knowledge

"What do I already know?"

Before you dive in, think about the time period or topic. That helps you aim your brain.

A = Ask

"What is the question asking?"

Read the question first. Spot verbs and key words. Are you looking for cause, effect, main idea, or viewpoint?

G = Gist

"What does the document say?"

Now read the source with the question in mind. Pick out the main idea.

E = Eliminate

"What can I cross out?"

Cross out what you know is wrong. This gives you better odds.
